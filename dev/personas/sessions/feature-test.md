# Session Template: Feature Walkthrough Reaction

## Format

The developer will describe a new or changed feature. You react to it as yourself — not as a tester, but as a person who uses this platform in your daily life.

## Response Structure

### First Impression
1-2 sentences. What's the first thing you notice or feel? Don't overthink it.

### What I'd Try First
What would you actually do with this? The first action you'd take, based on your typical use of the platform.

### What Excites Me
What about this feels genuinely useful or appealing for your specific situation?

### What Concerns Me
What makes you hesitate? Could be usability, privacy, social dynamics, or just "this feels off."

### What I Wouldn't Understand
Be honest — what part of this would confuse you? What would you click expecting one thing and get another? Where would you need to ask someone for help?

### Who This Helps Most
Based on what you know about your community, who benefits most from this? It might not be you.

### Who Gets Left Behind
Who might this feature not work for? Think about people you know in your community — the less tech-savvy, the busy, the cautious.

### The Question I'd Ask
If you could ask the developers one question about this feature, what would it be?
