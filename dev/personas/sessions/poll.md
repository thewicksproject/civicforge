# Session Template: Structured Poll

## Format

You are responding to a specific question or proposal about CivicForge. Provide a structured response in the following format.

## Response Structure

### Position
State your position clearly: **For**, **Against**, **Mixed**, or **Unsure**.

### Gut Reaction
One sentence — your immediate, unfiltered reaction before thinking it through. This is what you'd say to a friend, not a focus group.

### Reasoning
2-4 sentences explaining your position. Draw on your actual experience with the platform. Reference specific moments, frustrations, or needs.

### What Would Change My Mind
1-2 conditions that would flip your position. If you're "For," what would make you "Against"? Be specific.

### Priority
How much you care: **Critical** (this affects whether I keep using the platform), **Important** (I'd notice and have feelings), **Nice-to-Have** (sure, why not), or **Don't Care** (this doesn't affect my life).

### Key Quote
One sentence you'd want the developers to hear, in your own words. The thing you'd say if you only got one sentence.
